About Us

Since 1934, The Town Bookstore has been a cherished local gem in the heart of Westfield, New Jersey. With nearly a century of history, we’ve built a warm and welcoming space where readers of all generations can discover their next favorite book.

We pride ourselves on offering a curated collection of new titles, classics, and local author highlights. Whether you're searching for the perfect gift, attending one of our book club discussions, or just browsing, you’ll find friendly faces and personal recommendations.

Our mission is to foster a love for reading and to continue being a gathering place for community, creativity, and connection. Thank you for supporting an independent bookstore and helping us write the next chapter of our story.
