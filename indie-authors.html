Independent Author Policy

At The Town Bookstore, we are passionate about supporting independent and local authors. We believe in fostering a diverse and vibrant literary community where new voices can be heard and celebrated.

If you are an independent author interested in having your book considered for our store, please review our submission guidelines below.

Submission Guidelines

  • Submit a print copy of your book (or digital format upon request) along with a brief author bio.
  • We accept fiction, non-fiction, poetry, childrenâ€™s books, and local interest titles.
  • Please include your contact information and any relevant marketing materials.
  • All submissions will be reviewed within 4-6 weeks. Due to space limitations, we cannot guarantee stocking all titles.
  • We prioritize unique and well-crafted books that fit the tastes of our community.

Send submissions or questions to lisa@townbookstore.com. We look forward to reading your work!

Thank you for considering The Town Bookstore as a partner in your publishing journey.
